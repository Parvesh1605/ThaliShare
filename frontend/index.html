How the connection works

Three simple steps, built around how hotels actually operate — fast, time-boxed, and low-effort for kitchen staff.
1

Hotel posts surplus

After a banquet or buffet, staff logs in and posts what's left — food type, quantity, and a pickup window (usually 1-3 hours).

2

Nearby NGOs get notified

Verified NGOs operating in that city see the post instantly and claim it — first to confirm gets the pickup.

3

Food moves, impact is tracked

The NGO picks up within the window. The hotel gets a running "meals rescued" report — useful for CSR and sustainability reporting.
